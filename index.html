Essencse

  • High Concept: A very artistic game where players will get to mess and experiment with different colors.
  • Player Experience Goals: The goal of this protoype is to try and experiment with the particle emitter physics of phaser.
  • Genre: Essence is of the art genre. The game is made to be very sensually pleasing.
  • Game play: Players will click and interact with several colors to mix and match them into a beauftiful scenery.
  • Prototype goal: The goal of this protoype is to test how well the particle emitters work.
  • Features: A very visually appealing game players should have a sense of tranquility while playing Essence. The beauftiful colors are what really brings a player in to playing Essence.
  • Setting/Premise: Unlike most games Essence takes place ina wierd tranquil region. The area does no contain much of a setting.
  • Story: Since Essence takes place in this wierd dimension the only real story going on is that the player is interacting with the strangness of this dimension.
  • Target Audience: Anyone of any age will enjoy Essencse. It is appealing to human desires.
  • Play time: Essencse can be played as long as the player feels entranced with the game.
  • Strategies: There are no strategies to Essensce besides the enjoying the appealing scenery.
  • Prototype Assets: Most images will be made in microsoft paint or photoshop.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Essencse is very similar to the Stanley Parables peace room. No other game is quite like Essencse however.
